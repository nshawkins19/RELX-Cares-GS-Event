Welcome, Coder!

Computers can only do exactly what you tell them, in exactly the order you say it. The instructions you write are called an algorithm.

In this activity you'll guide a helpful robot 🤖 through a maze to deliver a box of cookies 🍪 to a customer's house 🏠. Drag command blocks into your program, then press Run to watch it go!

  1. Step 1 — Sequence: Put commands in the right order.
  2. Step 2 — Loops: Use Repeat to do less typing.
  3. Step 3 — Conditionals: Use IF / ELSE so the robot can make decisions.
  4. ★ Coding Pioneers: Meet women who shaped computer science.

Each step has two mazes — and they get trickier as you go. The ★ mazes are the tough ones!
